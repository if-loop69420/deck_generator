The canonical baseof a vector space [$]V[/$] is a subset [$]B\subset V[/$], that is not only a base of [$]V[/$], but also has the following structure (each column is a vector):

[$]\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 & 0 & ... & 0 \\\\ 0 & 1 & 0 & ... & 0 \\\\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\\\ 0 & 0 & 0 & ... & 1 \end{pmatrix}[/$].
